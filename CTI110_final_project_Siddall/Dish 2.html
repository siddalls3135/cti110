Thai Cooking










Ingrediants
1 lb Boneless Pork Loin
2 Tbls Hoisin Sauce
2 Tbls Soy Sauce
2 Tbls Lemon Juice
1 Tbls Honey
1 1/2 Tsp Cornstarch
1 1/2 Tsp Sesame Oil
1 Tsp Red Pepper Flakes
1 Bunch Greenbeans
2 Tsp Vegetable Oil
2 Garlic Cloves
1 Red Bell Pepper

    Preperation Instruction

  • Cut meat into thin 1 ½ inch by ¼ inch strips. Set aside.
  • In a mixing bowl combine hoisin sauce, lemon juice, honey, cornstarch, sesame oil and red pepper flakes.
  • Mix thuroghly. To create a marinade
  • Add pork to marinade.
  • Refrigerate for 4 hours.


    • Cooking Instructions

  • Heat oil in a nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  • Sauté garlic for 1-2 minutes.
  • Remove from skillet and keep warm.
  • Add oil and pork mixture to skillet. Cook until browned.
  • Add vegetables to skillet.
  • Serve over noodles.
  • Cook rice noodles according to package directions.
  • Serve immediately
