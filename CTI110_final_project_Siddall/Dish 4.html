Thai Cooking










Ingrediants
1 Pork
5 Garlic Cloves
4 Thia Chilli Peppers
1/4 Cup Water
1/2 Cup Thai Basil/td>
5 Green Onions
3 Tbsp Vegitable Oil
2 Tbsp Oyster Sauce
1 Tbsp Fish Sauce
1 Tsp Dark Soy Sauce
1 Tbsp Brown Sugar
2 Tbsp Lime Juice

    Preperation Instruction

  • Cut meat into thick strips. Set aside.
  • In a mixing bowl, combine Oyster Sauce, Fish Sauce, Soy Sauce, Brown Sugar and Lime Juice. Set Aside.
  • Chop Chilli into fine pieces.
  • Slice oinion at desired thickness.



    • Cooking Instructions

  • Heat wok over medium heat. Add Oil.
  • Add Garlic and Chilli to wok. Stir fry for 30 seconds.
  • Add meat to wok. Sitr fry for 5 minutes.
  • Add sauce from mixing bowl.
  • Reduce heat and add water. Simmer for 3 minutes.
  • Add Onion and Basil.
  • Stir and serve.
