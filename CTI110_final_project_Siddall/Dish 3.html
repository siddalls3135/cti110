Thai Cooking










Ingrediants
1/4 cup cut cilantro
4 cloves garlic
3 Tbls brown sugar
1 Tbls dark soy sauce
1 Tbls fish sauce
1 Tbls oyster sauce
2 Tsp ground pepper
1/4 cup coconut milk
2lbs boneless pork butt
Wooden skewers

    Preperation Instruction

  • Cut fork into 1in by 2in strips.
  • Roughly chop cilantro and garlic. Place in bowl.
  • Add sugar, pepper, soy, fish, and oyster sauces to the bowl.
  • Mix until a thick paste forms.
  • Add coconut milk to create a marinade.
  • Place meat in bag. Refrigerate.
  • Thread pork on Skewers.

    • Cooking Instructions

  • Preheat grill to medium high heat.
  • Clean and oil grill grate.
  • Grill skewers until well browned. 3-4 min per side.
  • Transfer skewers to platter and let rest 5 min.
  • Serve immediately.
