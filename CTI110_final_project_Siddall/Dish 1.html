Thai Cooking










Ingrediants
3 Tbls Vegetable oil
2 Shallots, thinly sliced
7 cloves garlic, sliced
1 lb ground pork fat
2 Tbls sugar
2 Tbls fish sauce
6 Thai chilli peppers
1 lb ground pork
3 cup thai basil leaves
2 Tbls Dark soy sauce
4 Tsp Oyster sauce
2/3 cup water

    Preperation Instruction

  • Thinly slice shallots, set aside.
  • Chop chillies into small pieces. set aside.
  • Chop garlic into small pieces. Set aside.
  • In a mixing bowl add fish sauce, dark soy sauce, sugar and oyster sauce.
  • Add oil to wok and pre heat under medium heat


    • Cooking Instructions

  • Medium high heat, add the oil, shallots and garlic, cook 3 minutes.
  • Add the chilies and cook for 1 minute.
  • Heatto high, add the ground pork.
  • Add the sugar, fish, soy, dark soy, and oyster sauces.
  • Stir-fry for 1 minute.
  • Add the basil, and stir-fry until wilted.
  • Serve immediately
